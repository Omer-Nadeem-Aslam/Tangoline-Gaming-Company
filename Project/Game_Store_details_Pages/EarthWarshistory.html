About Earth Wars: History

Earth Wars: History is an epic strategy game that takes you on a journey through the most pivotal conflicts in human history. From ancient battles to modern warfare, the game combines historical accuracy with immersive gameplay, allowing players to relive and rewrite history as they lead their armies to glory. Experience the evolution of warfare as you command troops, manage resources, and make strategic decisions across different eras. With detailed campaigns, stunning visuals, and a variety of units and technologies to unlock, Earth Wars: History offers a deep and engaging experience for history enthusiasts and strategy gamers alike. Will you follow the course of history, or reshape it? The fate of the world is in your hands in Earth Wars: History!

System Requirements



Minimum System Requirements:

These specs allow you to play the game at lower settings with a stable experience.

  • OS: Windows 8.1, or Windows 10 (64-bit versions only)
  • Processor: Intel Core i3-2400s @ 2.5 GHz or AMD FX-6350 @ 3.9 GHz
  • Memory: 4 GB RAM
  • Graphics: NVIDIA GeForce GTX 660 or AMD R9 270 (2GB VRAM with Shader Model 5.0)
  • DirectX: Version 11
  • Storage: 20 GB available space
  • Sound: DirectX compatible sound card
  • Internet: Broadband connection for multiplayer (optional)

Recommended System Requirements:

For a more optimized and visually richer experience, these specs are ideal.

  • OS: Windows 10 64-bit / Windows 11
  • Processor: Intel Core i5-9700K / AMD Ryzen 5 3600X
  • Memory: 8 GB RAM
  • Graphics: NVIDIA GTX 1060 3GB / AMD Radeon RX 580 4GB
  • DirectX: Version 12
  • Storage: 30 GB available space
  • Sound: High-definition audio device
  • Internet: Broadband connection for multiplayer
